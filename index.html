What We Will Do For You....

Coming to you a from sometimes sunny village in county Meath, Summerhill Harvest are a family-run Bakery. Founded in 2002, we have been deilghtfully preparing bespoke treats and nutruitious breads to Dublin, Meath, and surrounds for nearly 20 years! From Cupcakes to Cup Games, theres no occassion or order too big or too small. We are happy to service large clients whilst staying rooted to our treasured community support, where our Donuts are the talk of the town!

Larger orders of Cupcakes, Donuts, Pastries, Breads, and cakes of all sizes can be made to order for delivery with at little as 24 hours notice. Our resident fully qualified duo of patissiers are bustling with creativity, and would love to create a beautiful signature cake for your next big event or occassion. Please feel free to take a look at some of their creations in our gallery, or if you're ready to order get in contact on any of our social media below!
